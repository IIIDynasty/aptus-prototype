Pro Tip

How to Choose the Right Skills

Always include skills that are directly relevant to the job you're applying for. Aptus uses your listed skills as a key part of your AI match score — so the more aligned your skills are, the higher you'll rank.

📌 Example

If the job requires spreadsheet experience and you haven't used spreadsheets before — don't add it. But if you've used them even occasionally, do add it. And if you want to learn it first:

📺 Watch: 5-Minute Excel / Google Sheets Crash Course →

Aptus guides applicants toward submitting higher-quality, honest applications — helping the right people get in front of the right employers.

How to Write Measurable Achievements

Recruiters and AI systems both respond better to specific, measurable claims. Vague language costs you ranking points. Use the formula: Action → Result → Impact.

❌ WEAK
"Managed a team and improved sales performance."
✅ STRONG
"Led a 6-person sales team, increasing quarterly revenue by 34% over 3 quarters by introducing a consultative sales approach."
